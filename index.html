Intro

welcome to Superhero Sanctum! When you enter the server, you'll notice several different categories for your roleplaying pleasure. First is the Important category, where things like the community announcements for important things that we, the staff, feel that are important for you. The 2nd category, where you can chat as well as enjoy some memes and use your favourite bots, is our main hub of the Sanctum where you can have some general fun!. After that we finally move into rp related items, where you can make your character and give him/her powers. Now the best part: The rp channels! Here our city is split into 4 districts as well as homes. Enjoy your stay and feel free to start a journey!

About

Hello and welcome to Superhero Sanctum! This server has been a long-standing project meant to bring the best of the role-playing world straight to you! The staff here has worked hard at bringing this dream into reality. It all started September 2019, when SS's founder, CrownJewel, wanted to make a community where people of all kinds could come and have fun, no matter the experience or knowledge in role-play. The place has come a long way, as an expert development process as been incorporated into the server. It's guaranteed nothing like the Sanctum exists as of now, through our extensive set of channels to help you be the best character you can be. Over time we plan to grow into one of the most active and enjoyed role-play servers on the platform. We'll frequently bring exciting events to the city and continue the thrilling story!
